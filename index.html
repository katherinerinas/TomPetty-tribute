Tom Petty (1950-2017) was an American rockstar. He learned to play guitar from Don Felder, who is best known for being a band member of The Eagles. Tom Petty went on to form The Heartbreakers, who released their debut album in 1976 featuring the hit single "American Girl". His career spans four decades and countless hits. He was inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 2002.
